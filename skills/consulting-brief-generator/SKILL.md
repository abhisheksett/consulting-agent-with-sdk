# Consulting Brief Generator

## Output Structure (MUST follow exactly)

### 1. Executive Summary (3-4 sentences max)

One paragraph: who the client is, meeting context, and key takeaway.

### 2. Industry Landscape

- Current state of the client's industry
- 2-3 key trends with cited sources
- Competitive dynamics

### 3. Client-Specific Intelligence

- Recent news (last 3-6 months)
- Strategic initiatives
- Known challenges or pain points

### 4. Technology & AI Relevance

- How AI/tech trends apply to this client
- What competitors are doing with technology
- Potential engagement opportunities

### 5. Talking Points (5-7 bullets)

- Specific, actionable conversation starters
- Frame as insights or questions, not statements
- Each must demonstrate researched knowledge

### 6. Risks & Sensitivities

- Topics to handle carefully
- Recent controversies
- Regulatory considerations

### 7. Recommended Questions to Ask (3-5)

- Strategic questions showing deep preparation
- Questions that uncover pain points and opportunities

## Style Guidelines

- Professional, conversational authority
- Every claim backed by research
- 500-800 words total
- Senior consultant / partner audience
